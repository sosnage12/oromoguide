What is Oromia?

Oromia is the largest region in Ethiopia, filled with breathtaking landscapes, rich cultures, and warm communities.

Why Visit Oromia?

  • Wonders: Bale Mountains, Lake Langano
  • Cultural Festivals: Irreecha, Oromo New Year
  • Food: Ittoo, Oromo Coffee Ceremony
  • People: Friendly & Hospitable
  • Adventures: Hiking, caves, wildlife tours
